Hosted images - links stay clickable
Use “Copy signature” to keep links clickable (phone, email, website & socials all work). The Download PNG is a picture, so its links are not clickable - only use it if pasting ever fails.
  1. Click Copy signature.
  2. Open your signature editor - New Outlook: Settings → Mail → Compose and reply → Signature. Gmail: Settings → General → Signature. Apple Mail: Settings → Signatures.
  3. Name it, click into the box, paste (⌘V / Ctrl+V), set as default, and Save.
Note: links don't open while you're editing/composing - they become clickable for the person who receives your email. If a client strips the paste, use Download .html and open/import that file.
